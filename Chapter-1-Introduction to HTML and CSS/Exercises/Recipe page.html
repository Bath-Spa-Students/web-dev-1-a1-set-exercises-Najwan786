CHOCOLATE COFFEE

Indulge in the rich fusion of smooth chocolate and bold coffee with our decadent Chocolate Coffee blend. Crafted from the finest cocoa beans and premium coffee beans, this irresistible concoction delivers a harmonious balance of deep, velvety chocolate notes and the robust aroma of freshly brewed coffee. Whether you crave a comforting treat to start your day or a luxurious pick-me-up in the afternoon, our Chocolate Coffee is sure to satisfy your cravings and elevate your coffee experience to new heights. Enjoy it hot or iced, and let each sip transport you to a blissful state of indulgence.

  • Time: 8min
  • Serves: 2

    Ingredients:

    200 gm Dark Chocolate

    4 cup Milk

    1/2 cup Whipped cream

    1 teaspoon Chocolate chips

    4 teaspoon Coffee powder

    4 teaspoon Sugar

    4 cubes Ice cubes

    Step 1: Prepare the chocolate mix Break the chocolate into small pieces. Put them in a bowl. Add one cup of milk, coffee powder and sugar. Stir everything together.

    Step 2: Microwave the mixture Keep the bowl in the microwave oven on high (100%) for one minute. Take the bowl out and mix well.

    Step 3: Blend the ingredients Put the mixture in a blender and blend. Add remaining milk and some ice cubes and blend again.

    Step 4: Top it with whipped cream and add chocolate chips Pour the mixture into glasses. Put some whipped cream and chocolate chips on top.

    Step 5: Ready to be served Serve immediately.
