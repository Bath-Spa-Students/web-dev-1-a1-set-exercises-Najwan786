Plot

In a future where Earth is becoming increasingly uninhabitable due to environmental degradation, a former NASA pilot named Cooper (played by Matthew McConaughey) is recruited for a desperate mission. He, along with a team of scientists and astronauts, embarks on a journey through a newly discovered wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable planet for humanity.

Cast

"Interstellar" features a stellar cast including Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Amelia Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph (adult), Mackenzie Foy as Murph (young), Michael Caine as Professor Brand, and Matt Damon as Dr. Mann.
